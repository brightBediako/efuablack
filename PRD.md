# PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

## Project Title: Efua Black Official Website  
## Product Type: Artist + Ministry Hybrid Platform  
## Version: 1.0  
## Date: March 2026  

---

## 1. Product Overview

### Objective
Build a modern, high-performance website that positions Efua Black as:
- A leading gospel musician
- A worship minister and spiritual voice
- A bookable personality for events and ministry engagements

The platform must:
- Showcase music and collaborations (e.g., “Testify”)
- Enable event bookings
- Deliver ministry content
- Drive audience engagement and growth

---

## 2. Success Metrics (KPIs)

- ≥ 5,000 monthly visitors within 3 months
- ≥ 10 booking inquiries per month
- ≥ 15% conversion rate on homepage CTA
- ≥ 1,000 email subscribers in 6 months
- Avg. session duration ≥ 2 minutes

---

## 3. Target Users

### Primary Users
- Gospel music listeners
- Church leaders / event organizers
- Worshippers seeking spiritual content

### Secondary Users
- Media/bloggers
- Collaborating artists

---

## 4. Core Features & Functional Requirements

### 4.1 Homepage
- Hero section with latest release
- Embedded YouTube video
- Short bio snippet
- Events preview
- Featured songs
- Email subscription CTA

### 4.2 About Page
- Full biography
- Ministry focus
- Collaborations
- Professional photos

### 4.3 Music Page
- Song listings
- Individual song pages
- Streaming links
- Lyrics

### 4.4 Ministry Page
- Devotionals
- Video ministrations
- Testimonies
- Outreach activities

### 4.5 Events Page
- Upcoming events
- Archive (optional)
- Booking CTA

### 4.6 Booking System
Fields:
- Name
- Organization
- Email
- Phone
- Event Type
- Date
- Location
- Message

Backend:
- Store in database
- Email notifications
- Auto-response

### 4.7 Media Gallery
- Images and videos
- Grid layout
- Lightbox

### 4.8 Contact Page
- Contact form
- Social links
- Email and phone

### 4.9 Email Subscription
- Email capture
- Integration (Mailchimp or Brevo)
